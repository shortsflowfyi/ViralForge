# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 8, 2026**

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how ViralForge ("the App") handles your information when you connect your TikTok account.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you authorize this App via TikTok, we collect and store:

- **OAuth access token** – used to publish videos to your TikTok account
- **OAuth refresh token** – used to maintain your session without re-authentication

We do **not** collect:
- Your TikTok username or profile information
- Your followers, following, or video data
- Any personally identifiable information beyond what is required for authentication

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The OAuth tokens are used solely to:

- Upload and publish videos to your TikTok account on your behalf
- Refresh authentication when the access token expires

## 4. Data Storage

- Tokens are stored locally on your own device/server.
- No data is transmitted to or stored on any third-party server operated by this App.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

## 6. Data Retention

You can revoke access at any time by:

1. Visiting [TikTok's Connected Apps](https://www.tiktok.com/setting/) settings
2. Revoking access to ViralForge
3. Deleting the locally stored token file (`config/tiktok_token.json`)

## 7. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with:

- **TikTok API** – governed by [TikTok's Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/privacy-policy/en)

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer via GitHub Issues.
